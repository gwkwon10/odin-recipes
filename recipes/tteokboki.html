Tteokboki

Description

Tteokboki is the most classic Korean street food. It contains rice cakes, fish cakes, and vegatables drowned into a spicy sauce

Ingredients

  • Anchivy stock
  • Rice cakes
  • Fish cakes
  • Green onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Gochugaru
  • Garlic
  • Rice syrup
  • Gochujang

Steps

  1. Put rice cakes into warm water for about 10 minutes
  2. Boil anchovy stock
  3. Add soften rice cakes, soy sauce, sugar, gochugaru, garlic, rice syrup, and gochujang
  4. Cook for about 10 minutes until sauce is reduced
  5. Add fish cakes and green onions and cook for 2 minutes
