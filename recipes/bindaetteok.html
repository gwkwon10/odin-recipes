Bindaetteok

Description

Bindaetteok is a popular traditional Korean street food. It is a mung bean pancake that contains kimchi, pork, rice, and a variety of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Dried skinned mung beans
  • Sweet rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Groun Pork
  • Nappa cabbage
  • Mung bean sprouts
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak for at least 8 hours the skinned mung beans and sweet rice
  2. Grind the bean and rice mixture with 3/4 cup of water in the food processor until creamy
  3. Add kimchi, onion, garlic, mung bean sprouts, pork, salt, black peper, and sesame oil into batter
  4. Heat skillet over medium high heat and coat pan with vegetable oil
  5. Add about 1 cup of batter into pan and spread into about 6 in round pancakes
  6. Cook until bottom turns golden brown and flip over to cook other side
  7. Transfer onto large plate and make dipping sauce with soy sauce and vinegar
