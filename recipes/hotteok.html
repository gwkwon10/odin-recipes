Hotteok

Description

Hotteok is a fried yeast dough pancake with traditionallly brown sugar filling. It is a popular winter street food in Korea and can be filled with different fillings such as cheese, nuts, japchae, red bea, etc

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Sweet rice flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Neutral oil
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Make filling by mixing the brown sugar and cinnamon
  2. Activate the yeast by combing warm water and sugar and let is sit for a few minutes
  3. Mix in the salt, oil, all purpose flour, and sweet rice flour into the activated yeast
  4. Cover the dough and let is sit for 1 hour
  5. After your dough rise, knead the dough and cover for another 30 minutes
  6. After 30 minutes, knead the dough, form a ball, and cut the dough into 8 equal pieces
  7. Flatten dough into 5 inch circles and put some filling into the middle
  8. Gather the edges and seal the dough around the filling
  9. Heat a pan on medium low and add oil for a shallow fry
  10. Place the dough sealed side down and fry the side down until golde
  11. Flip the ball and flatten the balls into pancakes
  12. Fry until other side is also golden brown
  13. Remove the hotteok from the heat and let is cool before eating
